ABOUT THE BEAT SHOW

About The Beat Show Radio

The Beat Show Radio is more than a station — it is a brand built on sound, culture, and connection. Since 2019, we have been broadcasting powerful music that moves people, empowers artists, and amplifies voices that deserve to be heard.

As a fully digital platform under KTBS-DB (Digital Broadcasting), The Beat Show Radio stands at the crossroads of independent artistry and mainstream quality. We deliver the hottest blends of hip-hop, R&B, neo-soul, southern soul, and pop, creating a space where both rising and established artists can shine without compromise.

Our Mission

To serve as a bold and authentic voice for the culture, showcasing the best of independent music while connecting communities through creativity, rhythm, and real stories.

Our Vibe

We are powered by passion, guided by integrity, and focused on pushing boundaries — from the beats we spin to the movements we support. Whether you are tuning in for a fresh new sound, artist interviews, or exclusive drops, The Beat Show Radio is your front-row seat to the future of music.

Our Reach

From live radio and curated playlists to mobile streaming and community events, The Beat Show Radio is a multimedia brand that moves with the times — accessible, engaging, and always evolving.

The Beat Show Radio is not just what you hear — it is what you feel.
